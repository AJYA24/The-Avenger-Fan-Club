Take a look at the Infinity Stones, the main plot in Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.

  1. The Power Stone(Purple) has the power to give it's owner as much power that it wants. It was enclosed in the Orb before Thanos took it. It appeared in Guardians of the Galaxy, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame.
  2. The Space Stone(Light Blue) has the power to move the owner wherever he / she wants. It appeared in Marvel's The Avengers, Thor, Thor: Ragnarok, Captain Marvel, Captain America: The First Avenger, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame. Place: Asgard, Owner: Thanos, Odin, Thor, Loki.
  3. The Reality Stone (Red) can change the way a thing looks to another person (It can also make pemanent change) and it was some sludgy thing in Thor: The Dark world which was one of the three movies in which it appeared. The other movies in which the Reality Stone or Aether was present(Excluding Thor: The Dark world) are: Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.
  4. The Soul Stone(Orange) does not actually have any powers, but it obviosly has something that Marvel is hiding from us. Anyway, It is an INFINITY STONE!. It required a sacrifice to obtain it. This was the reason that two people(Gamora and Natasha Romanoff) died there for Thanos and Hawkye to get the stone. It appered only in two movies: Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.
  5. The Time Stone (Green) as the name suggests, can take the user to the future and move other people through time as well. It belonged to doctor Steven Strange, the current Sorcerer Supreme before it was taken by Thanos. It was concealed in the Eye Of Agamotto. The movies it appered in are Doctor Strange, Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame.
  6. The Mind Stone(Yellow) is the last and strongest stone. It gave powers to Pietro Maximoff(Quicksilver), Wanda Maximoff(Scarlet Witch), Vision wore it on his forehead before Thanos took it, and Loki also held the stone(at that time the Citauri Scepter) for the whole Avengers movie. It could control the mind of anyone who was touched by the scepter. It's original owner was Thanos, the Mad Titan himself who gave it to Loki. It appeared in: Marvels's The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Captain America: Civil War, Captain America: The Winter Soldier, Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame. Owners and Users: Ultron, Loki, Thanos, Tony Stark, Captain America, S.H.E.I.L.D, HYDRA and Vision.
